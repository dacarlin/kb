Introduction

SEEK is an open online resource to enable researchers and students to get jump-started in the field of computational enzyme engineering.

SEEK Beta 2

Fair warning: this is a beta release of SEEK. While data and methods should be reliable, count on things changing as we continue building the platform! Please email us with any questions, concerns, or comments. — The SEEK Team

explore the kinetic constants of the engineered enzymesLarge data sets

By using standardized methods we can generate large, high quality, data sets correlating changes in sequence and structure to measured enzyme kinetic constants. These data sets will further our understanding of the sequence, structure, function relationship in enzymes, and can be used for the development of computational enzyme engineering tools.

use the compiled data in your own research

SEEK aims to be a repository for the worlds largest data sets of kinetic constants from engineered enzymes produced, purified, and kinetically characterized using standardized methods such that measurements are quantitatively comparable

Simle robust methoss

Using the robust and simple methods provided in SEEK, researchers and students can rapidly and reliably carry out “design-build-test-learn” enzyme engineering cycles on a set of standardized enzymes.
